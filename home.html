The Directorate of Higher Education was constituted in the year 1996 by delinking the Collegiate Cell of the Directorate of Education and joining it with the Office of the then Under Secretary (Education), Secretariat. The Accounts Section was added with staff of the Accounts common cadre. Consequent upon the formation of a separate Directorate of Higher Education, all the functions of the Collegiate Cell of the Directorate of Education, except finalization of pension cases in respect of the teaching and non-teaching staff of the Goa University and of the Non-Government aided colleges affiliated to Goa University, were transferred to the Directorate of Higher Education. The finalization of these pension cases continued to be looked after by the respective Zonal Offices of the Directorate of Education.
